At EcoPlastile, we work with street children/ slum dwellers and other marginalized groups to collect and recycle waste plastics into eco-friendly construction products with application in numerous industries from paving to road signage to outdoor furniture. We use local labour to employ an existing technology of moulding plastic waste with sand, silt and saw dust to produce an innovative product and help solve pressing socioeconomic, health and environmental challenges of our time.
